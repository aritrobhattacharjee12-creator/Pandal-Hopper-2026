The Legacy

Since 1943, Ekdalia Evergreen has stood as a beacon of traditional Sabeki Durga Puja. Unlike modern theme-based pandals, Ekdalia focuses on the architectural grandeur of world-famous monuments, lit by the legendary Chandannagar light artists. The idol here is known for its serene, traditional 'Daker Saaj'.

The Vatican Reimagined

This year's structure is a meticulous 1:10 scale reproduction of St. Peter's Basilica, crafted entirely from eco-friendly jute and clay composites.
